Privacy Policy

At CineBook, we respect your privacy. We collect only the minimum data needed to provide the booking experience.

What we collect

  • Username and password for login and dashboard access.
  • Booking history and payment details stored locally in your browser.
  • Preferences such as notification settings.

How we use it

We use your data to personalize your dashboard, save reservation history, and keep your login state.

Data storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. It is not shared with external servers.

Contact

If you have any privacy questions, please visit our Contact page.
